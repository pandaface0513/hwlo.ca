Granville Island Pathfinding App

Design Concept:

The Granville Island Pathfinding App is a prototype done in a school group project. I was in charged of the programming and the layout of the app.

For colour scheme, we followed the colours used thoughout Granville Island, and its official website. The screen ratio was geared toward the iPhone, which was most popular during the time the project was conducted.

The button sizes were large enough for average person to select with ease. This is done through user research and the Fitt's Law.

The user can browse the shops on the Island, add each one to the dayplanner and navigate through the Island with the automatically map. With tourists in mind, as Granville Island is one of Vancouver's most popular tourist site, we have the languages tab, allowing tourists from all around the world to use our app in comfort.
