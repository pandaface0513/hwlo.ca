Mir

Design Concept:

This game demo was made during the iamgamer game jam 2013. The Game Jam consisted of 48 hours of not sleeping and be working under high stress to complete a game that boosts strong female protagonist.

It is about a robin hood esque thief with a variety of steampunk gadgets, including spring boots, stasis cape, a rubber suit, and teleportation chimney with smoke!

I learned how to allocate resources accordingly. In 48 hours, a lot have to be done. Therefore what needs to done first became a vital question, by prioritizing the parallax scrolling, the game became a pleasing artwork to look upon. However, the rest of the game were not completed.
