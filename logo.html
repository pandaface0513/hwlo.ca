SFU Aviation Logo

Design Concept:

The logo above is part of the branding design package for SFU Aviation Club. We really wanted to stand out as the only aviation fan club in SFU and we want the general public to recognize us immediately. Therefore, I chose an abstract version of an airplane. And then painted it blue, symbolize the sky that is above us. For the tail, it was painted red, as the SFU colour.

Why solid lines and brushed lines? First of all, we would prefer solid airplanes right? (jokes) The differences was to create dynamics in the graphic space, and to create the illusion of motions. Flames aren't solid, so the illustration of it shouldn't be as well.

Interesting enough, the resulted logo resembles that of the Vancouver Canucks, which helps aviators from other parts of Canada to recognize us immediately.
