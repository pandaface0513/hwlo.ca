Blocky - Music Visualizer

Design Concept:

This website is for a school group project. The project was to create a music visualizer in a 3D environment. The concept is have an array of blocks floating in space and dance to the music being played in the background.

The website features a full-screen 3D rendering. It might take awhile to load up, and once it loads the result will amaze you!

Shout out to Vivian=V+Ion who did the music, http://www.reverbnation.com/vivianvion
