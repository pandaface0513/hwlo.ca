SFU Aviation Banner

Design Concept:

This is part of the SFU Aviation Branding Package. It was a promotional poster that the club could showcase in an aviation fair or in SFU club's day. The photo in the background was taken in Pitt Meadows Airport.

The idea before is to show how it feels to be sitting in the pilot's seat. "The Sky is Yours" is the motto of the Aviation Club, and the club thrives to bring more people involved with aviation.

The QR Codes on the bottom is a test drive for those with smartphones to quickly browse the club's facebook page from afar. It provides the audiences a quick and easy way to get information from the Club.

Tools Used:
  1. Adobe Photoshop CS6
  2. QR Code Generator
