SFU Aviation Website

Design Concept:

This website is part of the SFU Aviation Branding Package. It was based on a template and I modified it to fit the branding of SFU Aviation Club. It follows the idea of being minimalistic and clean. The website was quick to navigate and it works reasonably good on mobile devices as well.

Unfortunately, the club is not hosting the website anymore. However, there is a live demo link on the bottom.

What I Learned:

I learned how it is like to work with a client that is not from my field of study. It was interesting that a designer will have to compromise his / her ideas with the needs of the client.
